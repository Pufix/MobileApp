The app will be a simple mobile app that reminds the user to take their medicine and to keep track of the pills stock 
and send refill reminders.

1. The app should send notifications begining at a set time in the day to remind the user to take their medicine.
2. The user should be able to enter the app and press a button that says that "I took my medicine"
3. The app should automatically decrement the pills stock.
4. The user should be able to modify the existing pills stock.
